Hungry Jack's Hack is a Mobile Substrate hack for the Hungry Jack's app that allows you to choose what prize you win among other things.

Free food. What more could you want out a Cydia Tweak?

Features:

  • Unlimited Checkins
  • Choose your prize
  • No Timing Out
  • Check In Anywhere



This hack is compatible with all devices with iOS 7 or later.
